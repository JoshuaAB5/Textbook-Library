About Us:

Our mission is to create a place for college students to help one another voluntarily. Instead of having others spend the long amount of time searching for cheaper textbooks they need as you do, you could save them the time by inputting the textbook you found into this library for others to easily find. While there isn't a benefit to the original person who found the textbook, you could help others save countless hours or dollars. Our library promotes helping others with no self-gain, which is a valuable attribute that would greatly help our society.
